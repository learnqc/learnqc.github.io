What Will You Get From This Book?

This book offers practical insights into quantum computing, accessible to readers without advanced mathematical backgrounds. Through hands-on simulations and coding exercises, readers can gain a deeper understanding of quantum computing and learn how to implement quantum solutions on real hardware.

Learn by reading

Reading the content in each chapter and the inline code. Complex concepts are visualized and presented in an intuitive manner.
Live Book

Learn by doing

Reviewing or executing the Jupyter notebooks in each chapter, and completing corresponding exercises.

Learn with an assistant

Interacting with UI applications and an AI quantum assistant that comprehends written and spoken language and presents visual responses.

Who This Book Is For

While this book goes into the details of quantum computing, it only uses elementary mathematics.

Software Developers

The book targets aspiring quntum computing developers. If you are a software developer with basic math understanding, you can learn quantum computing.

Applied Scientists

If you are a scientist who understands quantum theory, you can learn how quantum concepts translate to computing.

Curious Learners

While a hands-on approach to learning quantum computing is recommended, you could still use the book and its tools to learn how quantum computing works at a high level. In particular, the interactive mini applications and the AI assistant that complement the book are designed for less technical readers.
